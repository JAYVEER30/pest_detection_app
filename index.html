Detailed Pest Information and Control Measures

Pest Damage Control Measures
Aphids Weakens plant by sucking sap. Neem oil, insecticidal soap, ladybugs.
Whiteflies Yellowing of leaves due to sap feeding. Yellow sticky traps, neem oil.
Bollworms Feeds on cotton bolls, reducing yield. Bt spray, hand-picking larvae.
Fruit Flies Lay eggs in fruits, causing decay. Pheromone traps, remove affected fruits.
